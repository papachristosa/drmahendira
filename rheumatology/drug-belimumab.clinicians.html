For clinicians · Medication guide

Belimumab

anti-BLyS, Benlysta

Looking for the plain-language version? See the patient version of this page.

Bottom lineBiologic (a BLyS/BAFF inhibitor) added to standard therapy to reduce disease activity and flares in SLE, and used as part of combination therapy in lupus nephritis.

Dosing and administration[2]

For active autoantibody-positive SLE (and lupus nephritis). Available intravenously and subcutaneously.

IndicationStarting doseTitration and maximum
Systemic lupus erythematosus (IV)10 mg/kg IV at weeks 0, 2, and 4, then every 4 weeks.Continue as maintenance; reassess response.
Systemic lupus erythematosus (subcutaneous)200 mg subcutaneously once weekly.Maintenance weekly dosing.

Monitoring

  • Monitor for infusion or injection reactions and infection.
  • Assess for new or worsening depression and suicidality (reported in trials).

Contraindications

  • Hypersensitivity to belimumab.
  • Caution with severe active central nervous system lupus.

Key interactions

  • Avoid live vaccines within 30 days before or during treatment.
  • Combination with cyclophosphamide or other biologics has not been well studied.

Clinical notes

How it is used in lupus

An add-on biologic for SLE to reduce activity and flares, and one of the combination-therapy options in lupus nephritis.[1]

Common side effects

Infusion or injection-site reactions and increased infection risk occur; mood changes, including depression, have been reported. Described from background clinical knowledge and the Table 5 monitoring note; reconcile with the product monograph.

Tests and monitoring

Monitor for new or worsening depression or suicidal thoughts.[1]

Before you start

Screen for hepatitis B, tuberculosis, and HIV before starting, as for other immunosuppressive and biologic therapy, and address vaccination beforehand.[1]

Important cautions

Reproductive-safety data are more limited; individualise, with full guidance maintained on the pregnancy record. Avoid live vaccines during treatment.

+Reproductive health. Pregnancy, breastfeeding, and paternal guidance for this agent is maintained on the same record and is verified separately.
