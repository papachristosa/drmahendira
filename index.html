Why this exists

Careful answers, built to be trusted

Rheumatic disease raises questions that deserve more than a leaflet or a search result: whether a medication is safe in pregnancy, how long to stop it before conception, whether an injection is worth trying before surgery. This site answers those questions twice from a single reviewed source, once in plain language for patients and once with the evidence, grades, and guideline comparisons for clinicians.

Every clinical claim traces to a primary guideline or study, carries a visible last-reviewed date, and is signed off by Dr. Mahendira before it is published. Nothing here is written from memory alone.
