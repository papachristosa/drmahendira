For patients

JAK inhibitors

tofacitinib, baricitinib, upadacitinib, filgotinib, JAKi

Are you a clinician? See the clinical version of this page.

The short versionJAK inhibitors are stopped before pregnancy and switched to a pregnancy-compatible medicine, because they are small molecules that cross to the baby and there is limited human data. Timing of stopping depends on the specific drug.
  • Plan ahead so you can switch to a pregnancy-compatible medicine before trying to conceive.

When to call your team

  • Use reliable contraception while taking a JAK inhibitor.
  • Tell your team before trying to conceive so your medicine can be changed in time.
