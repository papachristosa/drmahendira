For patients

Anifrolumab

Saphnelo, anti-IFNAR1, type I interferon receptor antagonist

Are you a clinician? See the clinical version of this page.

The short versionAnifrolumab (Saphnelo) is a lupus medicine that blocks the type I interferon signal, which is overactive in many people with lupus. It is added to your usual treatment to lower disease activity and flares. Because pregnancy data are limited and it is not recommended during pregnancy, planning ahead with your team matters if you may become pregnant.
  • The decision balances controlling lupus against limited pregnancy data.

When to call your team

  • Do not stop or start anifrolumab without your team.
  • Tell your team promptly about any signs of infection, including a painful blistering rash (shingles).
