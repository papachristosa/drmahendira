For patients

Belimumab

anti-BLyS, Benlysta

Are you a clinician? See the clinical version of this page.

The short versionBelimumab is a lupus medicine that may be continued in pregnancy if it is needed to keep your lupus controlled, based on shared decision-making, since the data are still limited. It is considered compatible with breastfeeding.
  • The decision is a balance between controlling lupus and limited pregnancy data.
