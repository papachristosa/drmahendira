Tacrolimus

Prograf, calcineurin inhibitor

Looking for the plain-language version? See the patient version of this page.

Bottom lineBottom line: compatible across pregnancy, lactation, and paternal exposure (EULAR 2024). A useful pregnancy-compatible immunosuppressant, including in lupus nephritis. Drug levels may shift in pregnancy, so monitor and dose-adjust.
