Belimumab

anti-BLyS, Benlysta

Looking for the plain-language version? See the patient version of this page.

Bottom lineBottom line: EULAR 2024 lists belimumab among non-TNFi biologics that may be used in pregnancy if needed to control maternal disease, and compatible with breastfeeding. Paternal data are limited. Individualise; a registry-informed decision.

=Guideline divergence. VERIFIED DIVERGENCE.
