Medico-legal · Expert opinion

Independent rheumatology expert opinion.

Dr. Mahendira provides medico-legal consultation and expert reports in rheumatology, for plaintiff and defence counsel and for insurers, in medical malpractice and disability or impairment matters.

Services

What she provides

Opinion

Independent medical opinions

Written expert reports on rheumatic disease diagnosis and management, prepared to the standard expected of an expert.

Review

Records review

Review of clinical records, laboratory results, and imaging to form an evidence-based opinion.

Analysis

Standard of care and causation

Opinions on whether diagnosis and treatment met the accepted standard, and on causation in disputed cases.

Testimony

Expert testimony

Testimony at examination for discovery and at trial, where required.

Areas

Areas of expertise

  • Inflammatory arthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, psoriatic arthritis, and axial spondyloarthritis, including diagnosis, diagnostic delay, and disease-modifying and biologic therapy management.
  • Systemic autoimmune disease, systemic lupus erythematosus and related connective tissue disease.
  • Rheumatic disease in pregnancy, medication safety, disease-flare management, and antibody-related pregnancy complications such as congenital heart block.
  • Medication safety and monitoring, DMARD, biologic, and glucocorticoid therapy, including monitoring standards and treatment-related complications.
  • Diagnostic delay and standard of care in general rheumatology.

Her opinions are grounded in an active clinical practice as Division Head of Rheumatology at St. Michael's Hospital and Program Director for Adult Rheumatology at the University of Toronto, and as founding director of the Rheumatic Diseases and Pregnancy Clinic, one of a small number of subspecialty programs of its kind in Canada.

Case types

Plaintiff and defence

Malpractice

Medical malpractice

Alleged departures from the standard of care in the diagnosis and management of rheumatic disease, including delayed diagnosis and medication-related complications.

Disability

Disability and impairment

Assessments of functional impairment and disability arising from inflammatory arthritis and systemic autoimmune disease.

Balanced and independent

Dr. Mahendira accepts retainers from both plaintiff and defence counsel. An expert's duty is to the court, not to the retaining party, and her role is to give a fair, independent, evidence-based opinion regardless of who retains her.
