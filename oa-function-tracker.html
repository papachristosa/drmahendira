Knee & hip function tracker

Two short PROMIS® (Patient-Reported Outcomes Measurement Information System) questionnaires: Physical Function and Pain Interference, four questions each, validated in people with knee and hip osteoarthritis. Track your own trend over time, for example before and after a joint injection, and print your results to bring to your appointment.

About these questionnaires

PROMIS Physical Function Short Form 4a and PROMIS Pain Interference Short Form 4a, developed by the PROMIS Health Organization with National Institutes of Health funding. Both have been validated in people with knee osteoarthritis, correlating well with longer legacy measures. Each domain score is the sum of its 4 items (range 4 to 20); Physical Function is scored so that a higher number means better function, Pain Interference so that a higher number means more interference from pain.
