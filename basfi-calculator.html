Axial spondyloarthritis · Self-tracking tool

BASFI: track your physical function

The Bath Ankylosing Spondylitis Functional Index (BASFI) is a ten-question tool that captures how much axial spondyloarthritis limits your everyday activities. It is often used alongside BASDAI, disease activity and function together. Complete it, save your result to track it over time, and print it to bring to your rheumatology appointment.

How the score is calculated

BASFI = the mean (average) of all 10 item scores, each rated 0 (easy) to 10 (impossible).

Calin A, Garrett S, Whitelock H, Kennedy LG, O'Hea J, Mallorie P, Jenkinson T. A new approach to defining functional ability in ankylosing spondylitis: the development of the Bath Ankylosing Spondylitis Functional Index. J Rheumatol. 1994;21(12):2281 to 2285.
