Where to find us

The clinic is based in the Martin Family Centre for Arthritis Care & Research at St. Michael's Hospital, in downtown Toronto. Dr. Mahendira shares this office and reception with the Toronto Myositis Centre, led by Dr. Ophir Vinik, with a defined referral pathway running between the two clinics.
