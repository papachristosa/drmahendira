ESSPRI: track your symptoms

The EULAR Sjogren's Syndrome Patient Reported Index (ESSPRI) is a short, three-question tool that captures how dryness, fatigue, and pain have affected you over the last two weeks. Complete it, save your result to track it over time, and print it to bring to your rheumatology appointment.

How the score is calculated

ESSPRI = the mean (average) of the 3 item scores: dryness, fatigue, and pain, each rated 0 (no symptom) to 10 (maximal imaginable symptom).

Seror R, Ravaud P, Mariette X, et al, for the EULAR Sjogren's Task Force. EULAR Sjogren's Syndrome Patient Reported Index (ESSPRI): development of a consensus patient index for primary Sjogren's syndrome. Ann Rheum Dis. 2011;70(6):968 to 972.
